DISTRICTS of KERALA

Kerala has 14 separate districts, each famous for its own set of unique destinations and features. Following is a list of that can be used to gain information on each individual district based on your preference. This is to aid you in the process of gaining more data regarding the areas you would like to visit and plan your trip accordingly.

Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram is the 'Capital City of Kerala'.

Kollam

Kollam is the capital of 'Kerala's cashew industry'.

Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta is known as the 'Pilgrim Capital of Kerala'.

Alappuzha

Alappuzha is referred as 'The Venice of the East'.

Kottayam

Kottayam is the 'Letter Capital of Kerala'.

Idukki

Idukki is referred as 'The Spice Garden of Kerala'

Ernakulam

Ernakulam is known as 'The Queen of the Arabian Sea'.

Thrissur

Thrissur is the 'Cultural Capital of Kerala'.

Palakkad

Palakkad is known as 'The Granary of Kerala'.

Malappuram

Malappuram literally means 'hilltop' or 'terraced place above the hill'

Kozhikode

Kozhikode is reffered as 'City of Spices'.

Wayanad

Wayanad is referred as 'The Land of Paddy Fields'.

Kannur

Kannur is 'The City of Looms and Lores'.

Kasargod

Kasaragod is known as 'The Land of Gods & Rivers'.
